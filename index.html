Rana Alotaibi

I am a Senior Scientist in Microsoft Gray Systems Lab (GSL), working at the intersection of database theory and big data management systems. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of California, San Diego, under the supervision of Prof.Alin Deutsch. While at UCSD, I was a member of the Database Lab and Center for Networked Systems. My research focuses on semantic query optimizations in hybrid stores, solutions for enabling unified query optimization across different engines, languages/models for integrating disparate data, and scalable self-tuning big data management systems.
